FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: Do you have vegetarian options?
A: Yes, we have 8 vegetarian dishes including mushroom risotto and eggplant parmesan.

Q: Is there parking available?
A: Valet parking available at $10. Street parking also available after 6 PM.

Q: Can I bring my own cake?
A: Yes, with $25 cakeage fee.

Q: Do you accommodate food allergies?
A: Yes, inform staff. We have gluten-free and nut-free options.

Q: What's your dress code?
A: Smart casual. No shorts or flip-flops after 6 PM.

Q: Is the restaurant wheelchair accessible?
A: Yes, we have ramp access and accessible restrooms.

Q: Do you have kids menu?
A: Yes, for children under 12. Includes pasta, chicken, fish options.

Q: Can I modify a booking?
A: Yes, call or use online portal. Changes subject to availability.
